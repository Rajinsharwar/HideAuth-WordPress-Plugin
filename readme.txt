Introduction:
HideAuth - Hide the Author information, and Date published from your blogs pots with one click. Clean-up your blogs and make them look more fresh.

Description:
This plugin allows you to HIDE the Author's name and Published date of the blog. This plugin is helpful if you are the only author on your site, and don't want to display your name beside your posts. Simple activate this plugin, and the AUthor name and Date will be hidden, no extra options needs to be configured. Super light-weight plugin and easy to use.
